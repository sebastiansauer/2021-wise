Exam 1

  1. Aufgabe

    Which of the following statements about Switzerland is/are correct?


    1. Switzerland is part of the European Union (EU).
    2. The Swiss national holiday is August 1.
    3. Italian is an official language in Switzerland.
    4. The currency in Switzerland is the Euro.
    5. Zurich is the capital of Switzerland.

    Lösung
    1. False. Switzerland is part of the Schengen Area but not the EU.
    2. True. The establishment of the Swiss Confederation is traditionally dated to August 1, 1291.
    3. True. The official languages are: German, French, Italian, Romansh.
    4. False. The currency is the Swiss Franc (CHF).
    5. False. There is no de jure capital but the de facto capital of Switzerland is Bern.

    Gegeben seien die Mengen XX und YY mit jeweils folgenden n=5n=5 Elementen:

    Wählen Sie die korrekte Aussage aus!


    1. ∃x[x≥y])\exists x[x \ge y])
    2. ∀x[x≥y])\forall x[x \ge y])
