Exam 2

  1. Aufgabe

    Which of the following statements about Switzerland is/are correct?


    1. Italian is an official language in Switzerland.
    2. The currency in Switzerland is the Euro.
    3. Switzerland is part of the European Union (EU).
    4. Zurich is the capital of Switzerland.
    5. The Swiss national holiday is August 1.

    Lösung
    1. True. The official languages are: German, French, Italian, Romansh.
    2. False. The currency is the Swiss Franc (CHF).
    3. False. Switzerland is part of the Schengen Area but not the EU.
    4. False. There is no de jure capital but the de facto capital of Switzerland is Bern.
    5. True. The establishment of the Swiss Confederation is traditionally dated to August 1, 1291.
